Introduction

Chris Paul, born on May 6, 1985 in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, United States, is an American professional basketball player, a point guard, nicknamed "CP3", playing for the NBA Phoenix Suns

Honor

Chris Paul was selected by the New Orleans Hornets with the 4th overall pick in the 2005 NBA draft and played for the New Orleans Hornets, Los Angeles Clippers, Houston Rockets, and Oklahoma City Thunder in his career. and Phoenix Suns; a total of 12 NBA All-Star Teams, 4 All-NBA First Teams, 5 All-NBA Second Teams, 2 All-NBA Third Teams, 7 All-NBA Defensive First Teams, 7 NBA All-Defensive Teams The All-Defensive Second Team 2 times, the NBA All-Rookie Team 1st Team 1 time; the NBA All-Star Game MVP 1 time, the NBA season assists 5 times, the NBA season steals king 6 times, and the NBA Rookie of the Year 1 time. In October 2021, Paul was selected as one of the 75 greatest players in NBA history.
