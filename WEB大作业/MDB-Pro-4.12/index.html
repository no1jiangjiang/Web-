NBA Finals 2000

2000 was the first time Kobe Bryant played in the finals. 2000 was also the first time Kobe won the championship trophy. When Shaquille O'Neal fouled out, Bryant took over on the Finals stage. On June 15, 2000, in G4 of the finals, Kobe scored 28 points, 22 of which came from the second half and overtime. It was this game that made Kobe's hundreds of millions of fans remember Kobe.
